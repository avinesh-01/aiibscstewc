Warm Wishes and Support to Our Newly Appointed Honorable Executive and Managing Director, Mr. Binod Kumar Ji

  1. Home
  2. Event
  3. Warm Wishes and Support to Our Newly Appointed Honorable Executive and Managing Director, Mr. Binod Kumar Ji

It is with immense pride and joy that we, the All India Indian Bank SC/ST Employees Welfare Council (AIIBSCSTEWC), extend our heartfelt congratulations and best wishes to the newly appointed Honorable Executive and Managing Director of Indian Bank, Mr. Binod Kumar Ji. This prestigious appointment is a testament to his exemplary leadership, vision, and unwavering commitment to excellence in the banking sector.

Mr. Binod Kumar Ji’s illustrious career is a source of inspiration to all, embodying a journey marked by dedication, innovation, and an unrelenting focus on achieving organizational growth while fostering inclusivity. His appointment as the Executive and Managing Director comes at a crucial time, promising a future of progressive leadership and transformative initiatives within Indian Bank.

On behalf of the Council Team , we express our deep gratitude to Mr. Binod Kumar Ji for his assurance of full support to the welfare and development of the banking community, particularly focusing on the empowerment of our employees. His vision aligns perfectly with our mission to create a more inclusive and equitable workplace environment, ensuring opportunities for growth and advancement for all.

With best regards,
All India Indian Bank SC/ST Employees Welfare Council (AIIBSCSTEWC)
